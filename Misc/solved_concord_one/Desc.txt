Discord
Discord is a free social communication platform that allows users to communicate via text, voice, and video chat. Initially popular with gamers, it is now used by various communities to create and join servers, which are dedicated chat spaces organized into channels around specific interests. Discord is available on desktop, mobile, and web browsers.
